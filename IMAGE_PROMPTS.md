# Image Prompts for Laundry Services

Use these prompts to find appropriate images for each service. You can use them with ChatGPT, DALL-E, Midjourney, or search on Unsplash/Pexels.

## 1. Delivery Service
**Prompt:**
"Find or suggest a high-quality stock photo URL of a professional laundry delivery service. The image should show:
- A delivery person or van with clean laundry bags
- Professional, modern, clean aesthetic
- Bright, welcoming atmosphere
- Focus on convenience and service quality
- Suitable for a laundry pickup and delivery service website
- High resolution (at least 1200px width)
- Free to use (Unsplash, Pexels, or similar)"

**Alternative search terms:**
- "laundry delivery service professional"
- "pickup delivery laundry truck"
- "clean laundry delivery person"
- "laundry service delivery van"

---

## How to Use These Prompts

1. **With ChatGPT:**
   - Copy the prompt for each service
   - Ask ChatGPT to find free stock photo URLs from Unsplash or Pexels
   - Request direct image URLs that you can download

2. **Direct Search:**
   - Go to unsplash.com or pexels.com
   - Use the alternative search terms provided
   - Download high-resolution images
   - Save them in `/public/services/` folder with these names:
     - `delivery.webp` or `delivery.jpg`
     - `drop-off.webp` or `drop-off.jpg`
     - `self-service.webp` or `self-service.jpg`
     - `commercial.webp` or `commercial.jpg`

3. **Image Requirements:**
   - Minimum width: 1200px
   - Format: WebP (preferred) or JPG
   - Aspect ratio: Portrait (suitable for service cards)
   - Quality: High resolution, professional look

## Example ChatGPT Conversation

**You:** "Find me a high-quality free stock photo URL from Unsplash for a professional laundry delivery service. The image should show a delivery person or van with clean laundry bags, have a modern clean aesthetic, and be suitable for a website. Provide the direct Unsplash image URL."

**ChatGPT will provide:** Direct image URLs that you can download or use directly.
